Buy Me Coffee - Daap 

    A Decentralized Application that allows users to donate money for you and buy yiu a coffee 
    Once connected with wallet all the transactions are shown as it is transparent and more 
    
Dapp consists of 2 components
    1.Frontend - React,Tailwind CSS ,JavaScript
    2.Smart Contact - Solidity 

    To communicate between blockchain and React , we use Ether.js library 
    Help connect with each other 

Smart contract 
    Send Money to Buy Coffe and send a message and then an acknowledgement is sent
